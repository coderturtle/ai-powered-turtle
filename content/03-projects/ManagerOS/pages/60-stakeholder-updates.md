# 60 – Stakeholder Updates

Use the same sources each time:
- 30 Architecture (EU)
- 40 CTO/Platform
- 50 AI Enablement
- 90 Decision Log
- 99 Open Loops

## Stakeholder Set 1 — Your Teams (internal)
**Tone:** transparent, motivating, specific  
**Length:** 200–400 words  
**Format:**
- Wins shipped
- What we’re doing next
- Blockers / needs help
- Shout-outs
- How to engage

## Stakeholder Set 2 — EU Technology Leadership
**Tone:** crisp, delivery + risk  
**Length:** 150–300 words  
**Format:**
- Highlights
- Progress vs plan
- Risks + mitigations
- Decisions needed
- Asks / support

## Stakeholder Set 3 — European Business
**Tone:** plain language, outcomes  
**Length:** 120–250 words  
**Format:**
- What improved (impact)
- What’s next (why it matters)
- Any impacts / asks
- Risks in business terms

## Stakeholder Set 4 — International Leadership Team (ILT)
**Tone:** executive, concise  
**Length:** 80–180 words  
**Format:**
- 1–2 headline outcomes
- Top 2 risks
- Top 1–2 decisions / asks
- Dependencies (cross-region)
