# Learning & Upskilling

A structured, role-based, 12-month curriculum is essential for effective AI upskilling:

| Role                       | Months 1-3                          | Months 4-6                         | Months 7-9                     | Months 10-12                           |
|----------------------------|-------------------------------------|------------------------------------|---------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| AI Architect               | AI/ML fundamentals                  | Hands-on AI project                | Advanced AI certification       | AI ethics, integration blueprints      |
| Prompt Engineer/Developer  | Prompt engineering basics           | Small LLM-driven app               | Advanced prompt techniques      | Portfolio creation & LLM specialization|
| AI-First Engineering Leader| AI for Leaders course               | Pilot AI project                   | Establish AI governance & CoE   | Develop AI strategy & leadership brand |
